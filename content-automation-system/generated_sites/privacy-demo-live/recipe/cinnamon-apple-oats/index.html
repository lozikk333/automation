Cinnamon Apple Oats

4.9·275 ratings
By Chef·May 11, 2026
Jump To Recipe ↓

Cozy oats with tender apples and a warm cinnamon finish.

Prep Time5 min
Cook Time13 min
Total Time18 min
Servings4

Ingredients

  • ✓2 cups fresh vegetables
  • ✓1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ✓1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • ✓1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • ✓Fresh herbs to taste
  • ✓Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prepare all ingredients and place them near your work surface.

  2. 2

    Whisk together the dressing ingredients until smooth.

  3. 3

    Toss everything together gently until evenly coated.

  4. 4

    Taste, adjust seasoning, and serve fresh.

Notes

Try adding a splash of lime juice for extra freshness.

Use pre-packaged matchstick carrots for convenience.

This salad is best enjoyed fresh but can be stored in the fridge for a day.
